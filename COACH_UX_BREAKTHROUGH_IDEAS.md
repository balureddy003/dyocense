## ğŸ”¥ Controversial Ideas (Worth Debating)

### 1. **Remove Coach Selector?**

- **Pro**: Less cognitive load, AI auto-picks right persona
- **Con**: Users like control

**Compromise**: Auto-select based on task, show subtle indicator

### 2. **Make All Actions One-Tap?**

- **Pro**: Fastest path to value
- **Con**: Users may want to understand first

**Compromise**: Show preview tooltip on hover

### 3. **Hide Settings Button?**

- **Pro**: Cleaner UI, most users never change settings
- **Con**: Power users need access

**Compromise**: Move to gear icon in header, defaults work for 90%
