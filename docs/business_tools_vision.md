# Business Tools Vision & Criticality

This document defines the vision, criticality, and gap/problem focus for each core business tool in the Dyocense platform. All tools are designed as AI-first, agentic templates—leveraging conversational interfaces, contextual guidance, and automation to solve SMB pain points and drive actionable insights. Each tool is a flexible template that can be mapped and customized to any business type, supporting rapid onboarding and contextual adaptation. Conventional UI paradigms are replaced by chat-driven, context-aware, and proactive workflows.

---

## 1. AI Analytics Assistant

**Vision:** Deliver real-time, actionable insights through a conversational AI dashboard. Users interact via natural language to query sales, operations, and financial data, receive proactive alerts, and explore visualizations guided by an agent.
**Criticality:** High — central to platform value proposition.
**Gaps/Problems:** Fragmented data sources, lack of timely insights, manual reporting, static dashboards.

## 2. AI Data Mapping Agent

**Vision:** Guide users through onboarding and integration of new data sources using conversational schema inference, mapping suggestions, and validation. The agent asks clarifying questions, auto-detects issues, and resolves mapping errors interactively.
**Criticality:** High — accelerates onboarding and reduces technical barriers.
**Gaps/Problems:** Manual data mapping, schema mismatches, onboarding delays, non-interactive onboarding.

## 3. AI Connector Concierge

**Vision:** Allow users to discover, connect, and manage SaaS/ERP/data sources through an agentic interface. The AI recommends connectors, handles setup via chat, and proactively monitors integration health.
**Criticality:** Medium — expands platform reach and utility.
**Gaps/Problems:** Integration complexity, vendor lock-in, security risks, manual connector setup.

## 4. AI Goal Planning Agent & Playbooks

**Vision:** Enable users to set, track, and achieve business goals through conversational playbooks and agent-driven workflows. The agent suggests goals, tracks progress, and automates workflow steps based on context and analytics.
**Criticality:** Medium — drives engagement and business outcomes.
**Gaps/Problems:** Lack of structured goal management, manual workflow execution, static playbooks.

## 5. AI Forecasting & Scenario Agent

**Vision:** Provide predictive analytics and scenario modeling via conversational queries and agentic guidance. Users ask "what if" questions, receive forecasts, and get proactive risk alerts from the agent.
**Criticality:** Medium — supports strategic planning and risk management.
**Gaps/Problems:** Reactive decision-making, limited forecasting tools, non-interactive scenario analysis.

## 6. AI Team & Access Agent

**Vision:** Manage users, roles, and permissions through a conversational agent. The AI guides onboarding, configures access, and provides audit insights interactively.
**Criticality:** Medium — essential for scaling and compliance.
**Gaps/Problems:** Security risks, lack of auditability, onboarding friction, manual access management.

## 7. AI Insights & Recommendations Engine

**Vision:** Deliver personalized, context-aware recommendations via an agentic interface. The AI proactively surfaces business improvements, cost savings, and growth opportunities in conversation.
**Criticality:** Medium — enhances platform stickiness and value.
**Gaps/Problems:** Generic advice, lack of actionable recommendations, static insights.

---
*Each tool is prioritized based on its impact on SMB success and platform differentiation. Gaps and problems are addressed through modular, scalable solutions.*
