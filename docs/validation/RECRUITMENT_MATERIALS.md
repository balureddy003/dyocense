## Selection Criteria

### Target Mix (Total: 10 participants)

**By Industry:**

- 2-3 Restaurants/Food Service
- 2-3 Retail (physical + online)
- 2-3 Professional/Home Services
- 1-2 Construction/Contractors
- 1 Other

**By Revenue:**

- 2-3 Under $500k
- 4-5 $500k - $2M
- 2-3 Over $2M

**By Tech Savviness:**

- 3-4 High (uses AI tools, multiple software)
- 4-5 Medium (uses basic accounting software)
- 2-3 Low (spreadsheets only)

**By Engagement:**

- 5-6 Daily metric checkers
- 3-4 Weekly checkers
- 1 Monthly (for contrast)

### Prioritization Rules

**Must have:**

- Currently owns/manages a business (not planning to start)
- Willing to do video call
- Available during our testing window

**Strong preference:**

- Been in business 2+ years (understands pain points)
- Checks metrics at least weekly (target user)
- Uses some digital tools (not purely analog)
- Articulate and willing to share honest feedback

**Diversity goals:**

- Mix of male/female founders
- Mix of geographic locations (if remote)
- Mix of business maturity stages
